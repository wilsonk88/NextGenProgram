Why Nutrition Matters for Kids

Good nutrition is essential for children's growth, development, and overall health. A balanced diet helps support their immune systems, energy levels, and cognitive abilities.

  • Growth: Nutrients like protein and calcium help develop bones and muscles.
  • Brain Health: Healthy fats and iron improve focus and memory.
  • Immunity: Vitamins from fruits and veggies protect against illness.

Healthy Snack Ideas for Kids

Great snacks help maintain energy and focus. Try these kid-approved options:

  • Apple slices with almond butter
  • Yogurt with granola and berries
  • Carrot sticks and hummus
  • Trail mix (nuts, seeds, raisins)
  • Fruit kabobs with yogurt dip
