About the Program

Our mission is to transform lives by nurturing young talent, creating inclusive pathways to employment, and building a stronger tech ecosystem.

Join Us — Two Tracks Available

Alpha Labs — 6 Months

For a period of 6 months, selected young talents will be equipped with practical knowledge from leading industry experts coupled with leadership and interesting social activities. Participants will be given an opportunity to receive corporate level expertise and certifications from industry giants like Cisco, Microsoft, and AWS.

Seasonal Internship — 6 Weeks

This is an intensive 6-weeks summer program that runs in all our offices with the aim of coaching participants into becoming technical leaders or experts. At the end of each program the participants will be given an opportunity to present a real-life technology solution to a panel of judges. It promises to be challenging and exciting!

Eligibility

  • Recent graduates (under 2 years experience) — full‑time for 6 months
  • Current students — full‑time for 6 weeks
  • Full commitment to complete projects during the program

Our Objectives

  • Provide hands‑on training from industry experts
  • Build technical and professional competence
  • Address skills shortages in tech
  • Foster global collaboration and inclusion
